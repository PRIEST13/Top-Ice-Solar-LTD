Frequently Asked Questions

Solar energy is energy from the sun that is harnessed using solar panels. It is a renewable source of energy and plays a key role in reducing environmental impact.

Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity using photovoltaic cells. When sunlight hits the panels, it generates electrical current, which is then converted into usable electricity.

Solar energy reduces electricity bills, is environmentally friendly, and is a renewable resource. It also provides energy independence and increases the value of your property.

The savings you can achieve with solar panels depend on several factors, including your energy consumption, the amount of sunlight in your area, and local electricity rates. On average, homeowners can save up to 50-70% on their energy bills after installing solar systems.

The upfront cost of solar panels can be significant, but there are financing options and government incentives that make it more affordable. In the long term, solar panels save you money on your energy bills and increase your property value.

Solar panels are highly durable and can last 25-30 years or more with minimal maintenance. Over time, their efficiency may decrease slightly, but they continue to produce energy for many years.

While batteries are not necessary for all solar systems, they are a great option if you want to store excess energy for use during nighttime or cloudy days. Battery systems provide added energy independence.

Getting started with solar energy begins with a consultation. Our team will assess your property, evaluate your energy needs, and provide a customized solar solution. Contact us today to schedule your consultation and start saving on energy.
